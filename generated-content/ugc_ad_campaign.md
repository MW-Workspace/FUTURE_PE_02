# Katrina Cafe - High-Converting UGC Ad Campaign Pack

This ad pack targets remote professionals, creators, and students who struggle with noisy commercial coffee chains, poor food choices, and unstable Wi-Fi while trying to get work done.

---

## 🪝 Section 1: The Multi-Hook Matrix (First 3 Seconds)
A/B testing different hooks is crucial for paid ad performance. Use these 3 distinct variations with the same core body script:

*   **Hook 1 (The Call-Out - Focus & Productivity):** 
    *   *Visual:* Close-up of a laptop screen with lines of code/tabs open, shifting focus to a hand grabbing a perfectly layered flat white. 
    *   *Text on Screen (TOS):* "Stop trying to code at loud commercial coffee chains... do this instead 🤫"
*   **Hook 2 (The Sensory/Food Craving):** 
    *   *Visual:* Macro shot of a fresh, warm Cardamom Bun being gently torn apart, steam rising, glaze glistening.
    *   *Text on Screen (TOS):* "POV: You found the best morning pastry & study sanctuary in town ✨🥐"
*   **Hook 3 (The Relatable Pain Point):** 
    *   *Visual:* Creator looking stressed, tapping their chin while looking at a dead laptop battery or a spinning loading wheel.
    *   *Text on Screen (TOS):* "Unstable home Wi-Fi was ruining my productivity until I found this spot."
